Burger

Description

Buns meat and condiments

Ingredients

  • Hamburger patties
  • Hamburger Buns
  • Onions
  • Salt and pepper
  • Ketchup and mustard

Steps

  1. Put pan on medium heat
  2. Put patties in pan sprinkly salt and papper on them
  3. Flip patties when meat juices rise thru the top of the patty
  4. When burgers are cooked to your liking remove them
  5. Add sliced onions to the pan and cook untill carmalized
  6. Construct the burger
